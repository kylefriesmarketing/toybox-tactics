⌨️ Controls & tips
Left click / drag: select · double-click: select all of a type · Right click: move, gather, attack, build
Right-click a friendly toy: bodyguard it · right-click chest/tower/fort: garrison inside · B: town bell
WASD / arrows / edges: camera · Shift: queue orders · F: attack-move · Z: patrol · G: attack-ground
H: Toy Chest · . : idle worker · X: stop · Delete: demolish · ESC: game menu · M: sound · Ctrl+1-9: groups
F6: save game · F7: load save · Delivery Carts: right-click a Market to run trade routes for Buttons
Wind-up mice roam mid-map — walk any toy up to one and it follows you home for Snacks · Alt+minimap: flare
Each faction has a unique building & two unique toys · T: tech tree · , : idle military · ESC: settings
Win 3 ways: smash every enemy base, defend an Imagination Wonder, or hold every ⭐ Lost Sticker at once
2v2: pick 🤝 in Battle — solo you get an AI ally; hosting online makes it co-op (select a Chest to 🎁 tribute to allies)
